The 18-hole "Kenwanda" course at the Kenwanda Golf Course facility in Snohomish, Washington features 5,336 yards of golf from the longest tees for a par of 69 . The course rating is 64.1 and it has a slope rating of 101. Designed by Ken Harris, the Kenwanda golf course opened in 1963.
